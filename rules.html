How to Play

Back to Game

Objective

Be the first player to get rid of all the cards in your hand.

Setup

  • Each player is dealt 7 cards.
  • The remaining cards form the Draw Pile.
  • The top card is flipped to start the Discard Pile.

Your Turn

On your turn, play a card from your hand that matches the top card of the Discard Pile by either:

  • Suit (e.g., Hearts on Hearts)
  • Rank (e.g., 6 on 6)

Or play an 8 (Wild Card).

Special Cards

  • 8 (Wild): Can be played on any card. Allows you to specify the suit to be played next.
  • 2 (Draw Two): The next player must draw 2 cards.
  • 7 (Skip): Skips the next player's turn.
  • J (Reverse): Reverses the direction of play.

Drawing Cards

If you cannot make a valid move (or choose not to), you must draw a card from the Draw Pile.

If the drawn card is playable, you can play it. If not, continue drawing until you find a move.

Winning

The game ends the moment a player has 0 cards left.
